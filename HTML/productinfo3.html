YELLOW TEA



ABOUT

Native to China, Yellow Tea is another drink that has slowly gained popularity across the world.
This one is a little different in taste, as it offers a fruity and distinct after taste, smooth texture and a pleasing aroma.
When it comes to benefits, it is somewhat similar to green tea. However, it is easier on the stomach as compared to green tea and other teas as well.
The bright yellow colour of this hot drink is not natural and is attained through a process called ‘Sealed Yellowing’.
Under this process, the tea polyphenols (catechins) are first oxidized to attain the yellow tinge and then further treated to preserve the colour and aroma of the dried leaves.
